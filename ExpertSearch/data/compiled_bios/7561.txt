Biography 01.Biography 02.Publications 03.Articles & Press About Academic Groups Applied Economics Academic Area Economics, Finance and Accounting Charles Angelucci is an Assistant Professor of Applied Economics at the MIT Sloan School of Management.He was an Assistant Professor of Economics at Columbia Business School from 2015 to 2020, where he taught an MBA elective course on Competitive Strategy. Charles conducts research in Organizational Economics and Political Economy, with a particular interest in governance issues and news media markets.Charles Angelucci completed his PhD at the Toulouse School of Economics. Publications "Measuring Voters' Knowledge of Political News." Angelucci, Charles and Andrea Prat, MIT Sloan Working Paper 6132-20. Cambridge, MA: MIT Sloan School of Management, August 2020. "Newspapers in Times of Low Advertising Revenues." Charles Angelucci and Julia Cag. American Economic Journal: Microeconomics Vol. 11, No. 3 (2019): 319-364.
