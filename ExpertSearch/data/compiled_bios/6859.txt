Prior to joining Purdue University, Dr. Osswald held appointments as Assistant Professor of Physics at the Naval Postgraduate School (2010-2014) in Monterey, CA, and as Postdoctoral Associate (2008-2010) in the Department of Materials Science at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) in Cambridge, MA.
