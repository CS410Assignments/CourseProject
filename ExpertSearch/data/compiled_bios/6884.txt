lga23@eng.cam.ac.ukPublicationsResearch interestsLuis joined DigiTwin in January 2020 and works in the development of a methodology to determine the optimal use of computer models and data during the design stage of an engineering system. His investigation focuses on the propagation of uncertainties through a digital twin and decision making in the presence of uncertainties.
